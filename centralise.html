Analytics Dashboard Overview

The Growave Analytics Dashboard is your centralized hub to monitor, analyze, and manage energy consumption across your facility. Designed for institutions and industries, it delivers AI-powered insights, device-level control, and occupancy-aware automation—all within an intuitive, responsive web interface accessible from any device.
