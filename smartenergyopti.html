How Our Energy Controller Works

Intelligent automation that adapts to your facility's unique needs

Detect

mmWave radar detect human presence with precision, even when people are stationary. Environmental sensors monitor temperature, humidity, CO₂, and light levels.

Analyze

AI algorithms process sensor data to determine occupancy patterns, environmental conditions, and optimal energy usage for each zone in your facility.

Control

Automatically manage lights, fans, ACs, and other appliances based on real-time occupancy and environmental data, with manual override options when needed.

Advanced Sensing Technology

Our mmWave radar technology detects human presence with exceptional accuracy, even when people are stationary. Unlike traditional PIR sensors, our system can:

  • Detect stationary humans (reading, working at desks)
  • Work through partitions
  • Maintain privacy (no cameras) while ensuring accuracy
  • Operate in any lighting condition (day or night)

Understanding Smart Modes

Smart Mode

To enable fully automated, presence-based zone control across the entire facility for optimal energy efficiency and comfort in classrooms, offices, or labs.

  • Full AI-driven automation
  • Zone-specific control
  • Learns usage patterns

Priority Mode

To provide limited, controlled automation—only selected zones operate under environmental conditions for energy-saving in sensitive areas like VIP cabins or patient rooms.

  • Only critical systems active
  • 50% energy savings
  • Selective zone operation

Hyper Mode

To ensure maximum readiness during audits, VIP visits, or events by activating all lights and managing fans smartly across all zones on motion detection.

  • Full manual override
  • Event-ready configuration
  • Motion-activated lighting
